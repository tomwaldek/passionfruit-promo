05 — How It Works

FROM BRIEF
TO SCREEN

01
BRIEF &
MATERIAL

You provide the brief, source footage or titles list. We ask the right questions — tone, context, audience, and the single emotion the cut must land.

02
EDIT &
SCORE

We structure the narrative architecture first, then edit to picture lock. Music and sound design are editorial decisions — not afterthoughts.

03
REVIEW &
REVISION

Two structured revision rounds. Feedback is addressed with reasoning — we push back when a note would weaken the cut, and explain why.

04
DELIVERY &
HANDOVER

H.264 and ProRes masters, all formats your distribution channel requires. DCP references available for theatrical screen use.
